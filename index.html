About Dr. Demetry

Dr. Demetry graduated from the Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College in 2012 where he earned several academic awards. In 2014, he joined the team at the University Health Network (UHN) Comprehensive Integrated Pain Program at The Toronto Rehabilitation Institute. In this hospital setting, Demetry works with a team of physicians, nurses, occupational therapists and physiotherapists to diagnose and manage patients suffering from persistent pain. He later joined the team at the Pain & Wellness Centre in Vaughan ON, where they provide interdisciplinary pain management to patients of all walks of life, including those suffering from persistent pain.

Dr. Demetry was recruited in 2016 by provincially-funded agencies to educate Healthcare practitioners in their communities on the topic of persistent pain. He continues to teach his course Diagnosis and Management of Chronic Pain for Healthcare Providers across Canada. Dr. Demetry also lectures at the Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College in the department of Graduate Studies on the topic of chronic pain, and supervises residents in the hospital environment learning how to manage chronic pain.

In 2017 he was graciously awarded the Exemplary Service Award by the Ontario Chiropractic Association in 2019. When he is not working, Dr. Demetry spends most of his free time with his wife and son, making his wife cringe with his horrible dad puns.
